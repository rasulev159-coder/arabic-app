# Jobs-to-be-Done — Arab Alifbosi

## Core Job Statement

**When** I want to learn to read Arabic text (for Quran or general knowledge),
**I want to** practice recognizing and distinguishing Arabic letters,
**So I can** read Arabic independently without needing a teacher.

---

## Functional Jobs

### Job 1: Learn to recognize all 28 letters
**Job:** Distinguish each Arabic letter in all its forms (isolated, initial, medial, final)
**Current solution:** Paper textbook (Muallim Soniy), classroom at mosque
**Pain:** No feedback, no way to know if reading correctly, boring
**Our solution:** 8 game modes with instant feedback + spaced repetition

### Job 2: Stop confusing similar letters
**Job:** Tell apart ب/ت/ث, ج/ح/خ, ص/ض, and other confusable groups
**Current solution:** Repetition with a teacher
**Pain:** Forget between sessions, no targeted practice
**Our solution:** Weakness tracking + focused training on confused letters

### Job 3: Read words and sentences, not just letters
**Job:** Progress from single letters to connected text with harakat
**Current solution:** Muallim Soniy book, Noorani Qaida
**Pain:** No interactivity, can't self-assess
**Our solution:** Integrated Muallim Soniy textbook with quizzes per lesson

### Job 4: Maintain a daily practice habit
**Job:** Study consistently every day without losing motivation
**Current solution:** Willpower, teacher accountability
**Pain:** Easy to skip, no consequences, no rewards
**Our solution:** Streaks, XP, daily lessons, achievements, leaderboard

### Job 5: Read short Quran surahs
**Job:** Be able to open Quran and read basic surahs
**Current solution:** Memorization by listening (without understanding letters)
**Pain:** Can't read new text, only recite memorized
**Our solution:** Quran mode with familiar surahs, tappable letters

## Desired Outcomes (Metrics)

1. **Minimize time** to distinguish confusable letters (ب vs ت vs ث)
2. **Minimize errors** when reading words with harakat
3. **Maximize consistency** of daily practice (streak days)
4. **Maximize confidence** to read unfamiliar Arabic text
5. **Minimize dependency** on teacher/classroom
